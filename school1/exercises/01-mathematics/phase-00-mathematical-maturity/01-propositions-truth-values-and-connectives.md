## Part C - Conjunction And Disjunction

Let:

- `p`: `The drone has GPS lock.`
- `q`: `The drone has enough battery to return home.`

Write in plain English:

1. `p ∧ q`
The drone has GPS lock and
The drone has enough battery to return home.
2. `p ∨ q`
The drone has GPS lock or
The drone has enough battery to return home.
3. `!p ∧ q`
The drone has no GPS lock and
The drone has enough battery to return home.
4. `p ∨ !q`
The drone has GPS lock or
The drone has not enough battery to return home.
